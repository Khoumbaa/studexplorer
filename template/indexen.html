How do I use the map?

The map shows you various points of interest that could be useful to you as a student.

You have access to filters that allow you to display only what interests you.

Drag your two fingers across the map to zoom in and move your position, or click simply press the + and - buttons.
Clicking on a point of interest displays all the information about it.
