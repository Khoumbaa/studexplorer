What is the purpose of STUDEXPLORER?

The aim of ETUDEXPLORER is to help students find places that could help them with their studies or help them enjoy themselves even on a small budget. The purpose of the map is to show you the places where you can take advantage of student discounts, of low prices or free places to have fun, buy what you need and eat. We also offer you an automatic suggestion of places according to the type of place you are looking for and the city you are in.

What can I do if not all the points are displayed?

Check that there is no active filter on the card and reload the page. If the problem persists, please contact us by e-mail or on the networks.
